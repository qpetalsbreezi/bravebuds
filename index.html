About us

Our mission

At Brave Buds, we empower children across the SF Bay Area with essential self-defense skills. Our mission is to help kids build confidence and develop personal safety awareness through engaging, hands-on training. We offer programs at local libraries, public parks, and community centers, making self-defense education accessible to all.

Founder’s story

Brave Buds was founded by Saanvi Sharma, a fourth-degree black belt with 11 years of Taekwondo experience. Over the past two years, she has trained more than 300 children across the SF Bay Area in self-defense. Her passion for teaching stems from a deep understanding of how self-defense builds confidence, resilience, and real-world safety skills that last a lifetime.

Our programs

Our programs

We offer a structured 5-week curriculum designed to introduce children to the fundamentals of self-defense. Each session teaches essential techniques, from basic stances and strikes to situational awareness and real-life applications. Our classes are interactive, engaging, and tailored to all skill levels, ensuring every child feels confident and prepared.

Join us

Our self-defense classes are held weekly at libraries, parks, and community centers throughout the SF Bay Area. Families interested in enrolling can sign up online, and all registered participants will receive class details via email. Join us today and take the first step toward self-confidence, safety, and empowerment.

5-Weeks Self-Defense Program

  1. First Week — Building a Strong Foundation

    Event Details

    • Understanding personal safety and awareness
    • Learning basic stances, movement, and positioning

  2. Second Week — Striking for Self-Defense

    Event Details

    • Mastering powerful punches, palm strikes, and knee strikes
    • Targeting vulnerable areas for effective protection

  3. Third Week — Kicks & Evasive Techniques

    Event Details

    • Practicing front, side, and push kicks
    • Learning how to move, pivot, and create distance

  4. Fourth Week — Escaping Grabs & Defending Yourself

    Event Details

    • Breaking free from wrist, shoulder, and body grabs
    • Using leverage and quick reactions to gain control

  5. Fifth Week — Real-World Scenarios & Program Wrap-Up

    Event Details

    • Applying all skills in safe, simulated situations
    • Confidence-building exercises & final review
