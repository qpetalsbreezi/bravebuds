About us

Our mission

At Brave Buds, we empower children across the Bay Area with essential self-defense skills. Our mission is to help kids build confidence and develop personal safety awareness through engaging, hands-on training. We offer programs at libraries, parks, and after schools throughout the region, making self-defense education accessible to all. Brave Buds is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

Founder’s story

Brave Buds was founded by Saanvi Sharma, a fourth-degree black belt with 11 years of Taekwondo experience. Over the past two years, she has trained more than 300 students across the Bay Area in self-defense. Her passion for teaching stems from a deep understanding of how self-defense builds confidence, resilience, and real-world safety skills that last a lifetime.

6-Week Self-Defense Program

We offer a structured, interactive 6-week curriculum for kids—open to all skill levels—with essential techniques from stances and strikes to situational awareness and real-life applications. Over six weeks we build from awareness and movement to strikes, kicks, getting away from grabs, and safe scenario practice, so students leave with skills and confidence they can remember.

  1. 1

    Building a Strong Foundation

    • Understanding personal safety and awareness
    • Learning basic stances, movement, and positioning
  2. 2

    Striking for Self-Defense

    • Punches, palm strikes, and knee strikes
    • Targeting vulnerable areas for effective protection
  3. 3

    Kicks & Evasive Techniques

    • Practicing front, side, and push kicks
    • Learning how to move, pivot, and create distance
  4. 4

    Escaping Grabs & Defending Yourself

    • Breaking free from wrist, shoulder, and body grabs
    • Using leverage and quick reactions to gain control
  5. 5

    Real-World Scenarios

    • Practicing awareness, voice, and distance
    • Applying all skills in safe, simulated situations
  6. 6

    Program Wrap-Up & Confidence

    • Celebrate progress and build confidence
    • Review core skills and at-home takeaways

FAQ

Are classes free?
Yes. Free classes throughout the Bay Area at libraries, parks, and after schools.
Where do classes happen?
We hold classes at libraries, parks, and after schools throughout the Bay Area, not one single location. Details go out by email after you register.
How do I sign up?
Families can sign up online; registered participants receive class details via email. Use the registration form.
Who leads Brave Buds?
Saanvi Sharma, a fourth-degree black belt with 11 years of Taekwondo experience.
